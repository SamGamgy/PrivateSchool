Elementary Program

Montessori Lab School in Chesapeake, Virginia, provides first-rate elementary education with a strong focus on developing multiple intelligences. This is a full-day program, in a well-organized and fully-equipped environment that is ideal for sharpening your child’s problem solving and critical thinking skills.

Elementary

Age 6-11
5 Days / Week
8:30 am - 3:30 pm
Focus Areas
  • Spanish
  • Language Arts
  • The Sciences
  • History
  • Mathematics
  • Algebra
  • Geometry
  • Citizenship
  • Physical and Cultural Geography
  • International Culture
  • Performing Arts - Choreography

Designed for older children; our Elementary Program offers your child...
A well organized, fully-equipped environment that honors your child's multiple intelligences.
The opportunity to develop independent problem solving and critical thinking skills.
A wide array of challenging academic work in a safe and age appropriate environment.
The challenge of becoming a role model and teacher to the younger children.
An understanding of world culture and the celebration of cultural differences
