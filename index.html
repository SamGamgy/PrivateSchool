Our Areas of Focus

Pre-School

Ages 3-5

Montessori Lab School provides a non-conventional Early Childhood Education through our half- and full-day bilingual programs for three to five year olds. Our programs foster the love of learning, self-reliance and independence.

Elementary

Ages 6-10

At Montessori Lab School, we provide older children a full-day Elementary Education program that is designed to meet the intellectual, emotional and cultural needs of six to ten year olds.
